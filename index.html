Backup

Exports all your playlists (with track lists) and liked songs into a single JSON file you can store safely.

Restore

Choose a Backify JSON file to recreate your playlists and re-like your saved songs.

What's included
  • All private, public, and collaborative playlists with full track lists.
  • All liked songs are exported and re-added to your library on restore.
  • Playlists blocked by Spotify Development Mode are noted but skipped.
  • All data stays in your browser — nothing is sent to any server.

Backify runs entirely in your browser and only accesses Spotify after you approve.
